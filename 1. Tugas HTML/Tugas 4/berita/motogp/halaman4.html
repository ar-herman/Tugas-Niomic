Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third hightes on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGp) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-year old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - March Marquez (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
